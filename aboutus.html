About DoBu Martial Arts

Our Mission

At DoBu Martial Arts, our mission is to provide a welcoming and professional environment where people of all ages and skill levels can learn martial arts, improve fitness, and build confidence. We believe martial arts training is not just about self-defense, but also about developing discipline, respect, and lifelong health.

Our Values

We are committed to fostering respect, inclusivity, and safety in every class and activity. Our instructors emphasize positive discipline, personal growth, and creating a supportive community atmosphere where everyone can thrive.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by Mauricio Gomez, a passionate martial artist and 4th Dan Blackbelt in judo, DoBu Martial Arts has grown steadily to become a community hub for martial arts enthusiasts. Our experienced coaching team includes blackbelt holders across multiple disciplines and certified fitness professionals dedicated to your progress.

Our Facilities

Our gym is equipped with a large, matted martial arts area designed for multiple classes and personal practice. In addition, we offer a fully-equipped fitness room for strength and cardio training, as well as relaxation amenities including a sauna and steam room. Clean and comfortable changing and shower facilities ensure a pleasant experience for all our members.

Steam Room

Relax and unwind in our humidity‑controlled steam room—perfect for muscle recovery and soothing post‑training tension.

Sauna

Rejuvenate in our dry heat sauna, designed to improve circulation, boost immunity, and promote relaxation after workouts.

Fitness Room

Access a fully equipped fitness room stocked with cardio machines, free weights, and functional training gear to complement your martial arts sessions.

Changing Room

Spacious and secure changing rooms offer lockers, seating, and clean space to prepare before and after training.

Shower Room

Enjoy our modern shower rooms with hot water, privacy partitions, and fresh amenities for a clean and refreshing experience post-workout.
